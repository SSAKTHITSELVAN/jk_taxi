# Privacy Policy for JK Taxi

**Last Updated:** June 18, 2026

## Introduction

JK Taxi ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Name** - To identify you in the app
- **Phone Number** - For account creation and authentication via OTP
- **Email Address** - For account recovery and notifications (optional)
- **Profile Picture** - Optional profile customization

### Location Data
- **Precise Location** - We collect your real-time location when:
  - You open the app to show nearby drivers
  - You book a ride to set pickup location
  - During an active ride for navigation and safety
- **Background Location** - During active rides only, to:
  - Track your trip progress
  - Provide live location to your driver
  - Enable safety features
  - Calculate accurate fares

### Usage Information
- **Ride History** - Pickup/drop locations, times, fares, and ratings
- **Payment Information** - Payment method selection (payment processing handled by third-party providers)
- **Device Information** - Device model, OS version, unique device identifiers
- **App Usage** - Features used, preferences, and interaction data

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- **Provide Services** - Connect you with drivers and facilitate rides
- **Safety & Security** - Verify users, prevent fraud, and ensure safe rides
- **Communication** - Send ride updates, receipts, and support messages
- **Improve Service** - Analyze usage to enhance features and user experience
- **Customer Support** - Respond to your inquiries and resolve issues
- **Legal Compliance** - Comply with legal obligations and regulations

## Location Data Usage

- **When App is Open:** We show your current location, nearby drivers, and help you set pickup points
- **During Active Ride:** We track your location to:
  - Navigate the driver to your destination
  - Provide accurate ETAs
  - Enable emergency features
  - Calculate distance-based fares
- **After Ride:** Location tracking stops automatically when ride ends

You can deny location permission, but core features like booking rides won't work.

## Information Sharing

We share your information only in these circumstances:

### With Drivers
- Your first name and pickup location when you book
- Your current location during an active ride
- Your phone number (masked) for in-trip communication

### With Service Providers
- Cloud hosting services
- Payment processors
- SMS/OTP providers
- Analytics services
- Customer support tools

### Legal Requirements
- When required by law or legal process
- To protect rights, safety, and property
- To prevent fraud or illegal activity

### Business Transfers
- In case of merger, acquisition, or asset sale

**We NEVER sell your personal data to third parties.**

## Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures:
- Encryption of data in transit and at rest
- Secure authentication (OTP-based)
- Regular security audits
- Access controls and monitoring
- Secure API communications

However, no method is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

We retain your data:
